Gray Hat

A grey hat is a computer hacker, who may sometimes violate laws or typical ethical standards, but usually does not have the malicious intent typical of a black hat hacker

White Hat

White Hat hackers work with companies to improve their security by finding vulnerabilities and exploits that could be used by a malicious user.

Black Hat

A black hat hacker is a hacker who violates computer security for their own personal profit or out of malice.
